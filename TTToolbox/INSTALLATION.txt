1. Move or copy the folder 'TT Toolbox' to your local grasshopper components folder: C:\Users\[USERNAME]\AppData\Roaming\Grasshopper\Libraries
	-Type "GrasshopperFolders" in Rhino command line
	-Select "Component"
2. Start Rhino and Grasshopper and look for the TT Toolbox tab in the Grasshopper Ribbon.
3. Enjoy!
